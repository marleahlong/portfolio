ABOUT
2021
Publication design for SOLOS, a quarterly series of interpretations and re-compositions; a platform to present solo work from a new perspective with accountability to the original. I worked with the founders to create the first publication of the series and rebrand their identity.
2021
App rebrand for Yardsale Treasure Hunt. The deliverables of this project included logo variations, typeface and color palette, and social media graphics.
2019
Side Stand is exploration of the duality of home, adolescence, and femininity. Side Stand is the culmination of academic research, and a childhood spent in rural Florida. Comprised of a self made environment and two publications it allows viewers to question their own reality of home. System was completely built, designed, and implemented using techniques such as risography, various binding processes, and woodworking.
2022
Rebrand and complete overhaul of visual identity for Fuugly, a fun furniture brand based in Los Angeles that specializes in mirrors and other oddities. The rebrand included a new Shopify site as well as printed deliverables.
2018 - ongoing
Various commissioned posters made for VCUarts, Richmond Reproductive Freedom Project, and Richmond based dj duo Movement. Posters made by various print methods.
2018 - ongoing
Various projects created singularly and collaboratively by a numerous number of physical and digital processes. Includes work from Little Sip, an independent publishing identity, Urban Outfitters, Nicole Killian, John Huggins, and a long list of other artists and designers.
